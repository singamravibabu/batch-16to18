# Descriptive Statistics

## 1. What are Descriptive Statistics?
- Summarizes and describe features of a dataset
- In time-series and any dataset, these help answer:
    - What is the *typical* value?
    - How much *variation* is there?
    - Are there *unusual* values (outliers)?
    - Is the distribution *skewed*?
- The help understand data **before** modeling.
